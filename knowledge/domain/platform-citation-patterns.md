# Platform-Specific Citation Patterns

> **Confidence:** high
> **Last verified:** 2026-02-11
> **Source:** Yext 6.8M citations / 1.6M responses study, multiple corroborating sources
> **Updated by:** research — 2026-02-11

## Key Insight

Each AI engine has distinct sourcing preferences. A site can score well for Gemini but poorly for ChatGPT. Only 11% of domains are cited by both ChatGPT and Perplexity.

## Platform Profiles

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)

- **Market share:** 64-68% global, 59.5% US (Jan 2026). 800M weekly active users, 2B queries/day
- **Favors:** Wikipedia (47.9% of citations), established publications (Forbes), broad editorial mentions
- **Key behavior:** Negative correlation with Google rankings for product recommendations. Only 8-12% overlap between top Google results and ChatGPT-cited sources for commercial B2B queries
- **What works:** Third-party editorial mentions, consistent entity data across sources, "best of" list placements
- **Conversion rate:** 14.2%

### Perplexity

- **Market share:** 2.0% global, 6.2% US. 22M monthly active users, 780M monthly searches
- **Favors:** Reddit (46.7%), YouTube, niche industry-specific directories (Zocdoc for healthcare, TripAdvisor for hospitality)
- **Key behavior:** Highest citation density at 6.61 citations per response. Niche sources = 24% of all citations for subjective queries
- **What works:** Industry-specific directory presence, Reddit/community mentions, niche authority content
- **Revenue sharing:** Allocated $42.5M to publishers (keeps 20%, shares 80% of subscription revenue)

### Google Gemini / AI Overviews / AI Mode

- **Market share:** 18.2-21.5% global, 13.4% US. 400M monthly active users. AI Overviews in 18% of global searches
- **Favors:** Brand-owned websites (52.15% of citations — only engine that heavily favors first-party content)
- **Key behavior:** Strongest correlation with traditional search rankings: 93.67% of AI Overview citations link to a top-10 organic result
- **What works:** Structured/factual content on brand domains, proper schema markup, local landing pages, consistent subdomains
- **AI Mode zero-click rate:** 93%

### Claude (Anthropic)

- **Market share:** 3.2% US. 18.9M monthly users
- **Key behavior:** Highest conversion rate at 16.8% (vs ChatGPT 14.2%)
- **Status:** Less studied for citation patterns. Increasingly relevant due to conversion rate

## Implications for Pare Scoring

- **Platform-specific subscores are essential.** A single "AI Visibility" score hides the fact that a business might be visible on Gemini but invisible on ChatGPT
- **ChatGPT readiness ≠ Google readiness.** 80% of AI-cited sources are NOT in Google's top results
- **Perplexity requires niche directory strategy.** Generic business listings don't help — industry-specific directories matter
- **Gemini is closest to traditional SEO.** First-party content + schema markup + organic rankings drive Gemini citations
- **Claude monitoring should be added** despite smaller market share — highest conversion rate
