## AEO vs GEO Terminology

AEO and GEO are **the same discipline with different emphasis**:
- **AEO** = Optimizing for AI answer features (Google AI Overviews, answer snippets)
- **GEO** = Influencing AI content generation (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity)
- Industry consensus: "Different names for the same trend"
- **Recommendation:** Use "GEO" as primary term (more specificity, academic backing)

## Key Trend: AI Search vs Traditional Search

- AI platforms: <1% of global web traffic but growing 527% YoY
