## Industry-Converged Metrics (5 Core)

1. **Citation Frequency** — How often brand is cited across AI platforms
2. **AI Share of Voice** — Brand's share of AI mentions vs competitors
3. **Sentiment Analysis** — How positively/negatively AI describes brand
4. **AI Readiness Score** — Technical site optimization for AI citation
5. **LLM Conversion Rate** — Traffic from AI referrals to conversions

## What No One Does Well (Our Gaps to Exploit)

| Gap | Detail |
|-----|--------|
| **No unified weighted score** | Tools track presence/absence but don't combine technical readiness + live citation monitoring in one actionable score |
| **Shallow platform breakdowns** | Most tools don't explain WHY a platform cites or doesn't cite you |
| **Generic recommendations** | "Add schema" vs "Add Organization schema with sameAs linking to G2 and Crunchbase because Perplexity weights niche directories" |
| **Local business underserved** | Most GEO tools focus on enterprise/SaaS — massive SMB local market unaddressed |
| **No audit-to-implement-to-verify loop** | Competitors either audit OR implement, never both with built-in verification |

## Pare Competitive Differentiators

1. **Platform-specific scoring** with specific remediation per engine
2. **Evidence-based weights** referencing the 129K domain study, GEO arXiv paper, Yext 6.8M citation study
3. **Vertical-specific benchmarks** (dental vs HVAC vs B2B SaaS vs legal)
4. **Audit → implement → verify loop** (unique — no competitor closes this loop)
5. **Accessible SMB pricing** ($500-$2K audit vs $5K-$30K industry norm)
