# Domain Knowledge: GEO and the Pare Business

## What Is GEO?

**Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** = optimizing a business's online presence so that AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot) mention, recommend, and link to them when users ask relevant questions.

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's search ranking. GEO optimizes for AI engine citations. The two overlap but have different priorities:

| Factor | SEO Weight | GEO Weight | Why Different |
|--------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Backlinks | High | Low-Medium | AI engines weight content quality over link graphs |
| Schema Markup | Medium | Medium | Helps AI comprehend, doesn't drive citations |
| Content Quality | Medium | **Very High** | #1 factor in whether AI engines cite a source |
| Page Speed | Medium | Low | AI engines care about content, not speed |
| Answer-First Format | Low | **High** | AI engines prefer content that directly answers questions |
| Statistics/Data | Low | **High** | Quantifiable claims get cited more |
| Author Attribution | Low | **Medium** | E-E-A-T signals help AI trust content |

## Key Research Findings

1. **Content quality is the #1 citation driver** — 30% weight, not schema markup
2. **Schema helps comprehension, NOT citation frequency** — important distinction for sales
3. **GEO works:** 30-40% visibility improvements documented, 63% of optimized companies report improvement
4. **llms.txt has no confirmed crawler support** — implement for clients but don't sell as major factor
5. **89% of websites are unprepared for AI search** — massive TAM
6. **AI-powered search projected to capture 25% of global search market** ($72B in digital traffic) by 2026

## Pare's Competitive Position

### The Moat (What No Tool Replicates)
1. **Done-for-you implementation** — Every tool tells you what's wrong. Pare fixes it. The audit → sprint → verify loop.
2. **Branded PDF audit reports** — No tool generates agency-branded, client-ready reports programmatically.
3. **Vertical-specific schema generation** — No tool generates Dentist, Attorney, MedicalProcedure schema. No CMS plugin handles this.

Pare is a consulting business, not a software product. The software (this engine) powers the consulting. Don't compete with $29/mo tools on monitoring features. Compete on implementation quality and client outcomes.

## Target Client Profile

### Ideal Client
- Local service business ($500K-$5M revenue)
- Verticals: dental, legal, HVAC, accounting, restaurant, medical, real estate
- Has a website that's >2 years old (established but not optimized for AI)
- Not mentioned by AI engines when people ask relevant questions

## The Audit Report

The 9-page branded PDF is the primary sales tool and deliverable:

1. **Cover Page** — Client name, date, overall score with letter grade, Pare branding
2. **Executive Summary** — Top 3 findings, overall assessment, urgency
3. **AI Visibility** — How each AI engine sees the business, mention rates, competitor comparison
4. **Content Quality** — What's working, what's missing, specific page-level recommendations
5. **Schema & Structured Data** — Current vs. recommended, gap analysis, code examples
6. **Technical Readiness** — Crawler access, llms.txt, sitemap, mobile, SSL
7. **Local/GBP** — Profile completeness, reviews, NAP consistency
8. **AI Accuracy** — Misinformation detected in AI responses about the business
9. **Action Plan** — Prioritized recommendations with estimated impact
