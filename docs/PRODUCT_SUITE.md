# Product Suite & Pricing

## Product Overview

Pare offers four products in a deliberate upsell path. Each product naturally leads to the next. The audit creates urgency. The sprint delivers results. The retainer maintains and extends gains. The agentic commerce sprint captures the emerging AI transaction channel.

**Upsell Path:**
Free mini-audit --> Full audit ($750-1,500) --> Sprint ($3-7K) --> Retainer ($1,500+/mo) --> Agentic commerce sprint ($5-10K)

---

## Product 1: AI Readiness Audit ($750-$1,500)

### What It Is

A 9-page branded PDF showing how AI search engines see the business today. This is the primary sales tool -- the audit creates urgency and positions the sprint as the obvious next step.

### What It Includes

- **5-Pillar Scoring**: Technical Foundation, Content Quality, Authority Signals, Schema Implementation, AI Platform Visibility
- **Overall AI Readiness Score** (0-100)
- **Competitor Comparison**: Side-by-side scoring against 3-5 named competitors
- **Platform-by-Platform Analysis**: What ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot say about the business
- **Prioritized Action Plan**: Specific fixes ranked by impact and effort
- **Executive Summary**: One-page overview suitable for forwarding to decision makers

### Sales Experience

The audit is not just a deliverable -- it is a designed sales experience:

1. **Teaser finding** -- A single provocative data point sent via email or LinkedIn ("I asked ChatGPT for the best dentist in [city] and your competitor [name] was #1. You weren't mentioned.")
2. **Mini-audit (1-page)** -- Free automated summary showing their score and top 3 issues. Used as lead magnet.
3. **Full audit (9-page PDF)** -- Paid deliverable. Comprehensive analysis with competitor comparison.
4. **Walkthrough call (30-45 min)** -- Screen share of the report. Live demo asking ChatGPT about their industry. Sprint proposal presented at the end.

### Design Principles

- Lead with fear: "You're invisible to AI search"
- Follow with hope: "Here's exactly how to fix it"
- Make competitors visible: "Here's who IS showing up instead of you"
- Use their brand name: personalize every page
- Quantify the gap: specific scores, not vague assessments
- Make the fix feel achievable: "4-week sprint, we handle everything"

### Pricing

- **Standard audit**: $750 (single location, 3 competitors)
- **Multi-location audit**: $1,500 (up to 5 locations, 5 competitors)
- **Batch audit** (prospecting): Free/internal cost -- used to generate leads, not sold directly

## Product 2: AI Visibility Sprint ($3,000-$10,000)

### What It Is

A 4-week implementation engagement where Pare implements all technical and content changes needed to improve AI visibility. This is the core revenue product.

### Tiers

| Tier | Price | For | Includes |
|------|-------|-----|----------|
| Starter | $3,000 | Single-location, <20 pages | Core schema + 5 pages optimized + GBP optimization |
| Growth | $5,000 | Multi-service, WordPress/custom CMS | Full schema suite + 10 pages optimized + GBP + competitive report |
| Enterprise | $7,000-$10,000 | Multi-location, e-commerce | Everything + agentic commerce readiness + multi-location schema |

### Sprint Deliverables (5 Packages)

**Package 1: Technical Infrastructure**
- robots.txt configured for AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, etc.)
- llms.txt created and deployed (machine-readable business summary)
- XML sitemap verified and optimized
- Page speed and crawlability audit
- HTTPS and canonical URL verification

**Package 2: Schema Markup Suite**
- Organization schema (company identity)
- LocalBusiness schema (location, hours, services, service area)
- BreadcrumbList schema (site navigation)
- WebSite schema (search action, site name)
- FAQPage schema (on top 5-10 pages)
- Service or Product schema (per service/product line)
- Person schema (for team/providers)
- Review/AggregateRating schema (testimonials)

**Package 3: Content Optimization**
- Answer-first content restructuring on top 5-10 pages
- FAQ sections added to service pages
- Statistics and citation insertion (authoritative data points)
- Author bios and expertise signals (E-E-A-T alignment)
- Content gap analysis vs competitors

**Package 4: Local Authority**
- Google Business Profile optimization
- NAP consistency audit and fixes (Name, Address, Phone)
- sameAs links to all verified profiles
- Directory recommendation list (industry-specific)
- Review strategy recommendations

**Package 5: Baseline + Proof**
- Full re-audit at sprint completion (quantified before/after)
- Side-by-side score comparison
- Monitoring baseline established (25+ queries across 6 platforms)
- Documentation of all changes delivered
- 90-day performance guarantee

### Payment Terms

- 50% deposit at contract signing
- 50% at sprint completion (Week 4 handoff)

## Product 3: Monthly Retainer ($1,500-$5,000/mo)

### Pricing Decision

Original spec had a $500/mo basic tier. This was revised to $1,500 minimum because $500 is below cost when accounting for tool costs + API costs + 3-4 hours of work per client per month.

Exception: A $500/mo monitoring-only tier (automated reports, no optimization) is viable because it is fully automated with near-zero marginal cost. This is a retention play, not a services offering.

### Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Monitoring Only | $500/mo | Automated weekly monitoring (25 queries, 6 platforms), monthly PDF report, email alerts for drops >20%, no optimization work |
| Standard | $1,500/mo | Weekly monitoring (25 queries, 6 platforms), monthly content optimization (2-3 pages), monthly 30-min strategy call, new schema deployment as pages added, competitive intelligence updates, email/Slack support, quarterly full re-audit |
| Premium | $3,000-5,000/mo | Everything in Standard + weekly content optimization, bi-weekly strategy calls, agentic commerce monitoring, citation building campaigns, priority support, custom competitive alerts, executive summaries for leadership |

### What Retainer Clients Receive Monthly

**Standard tier deliverables per month:**
- 4x weekly monitoring reports (automated)
- 1x monthly trend analysis with strategic recommendations
- 2-3 pages optimized or new content created
- Schema updates for any new pages/services
- 1x 30-min strategy call
- Competitive movement alerts

**Quarterly:**
- Full re-audit with trend analysis
- Comprehensive report comparing scores over time
- Strategic recommendations for next quarter

## Product 4: Agentic Commerce Readiness Sprint ($5,000-$10,000)

### What It Is

A specialized sprint for e-commerce and service businesses preparing for AI-driven transactions. This is the emerging product -- positioned for businesses that want to be ready when AI agents start purchasing on behalf of consumers.

### Why It Matters

- Google Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) launched Jan 2026
- OpenAI Agent Commerce Protocol (ACP) launched Sept 2025
- Shopify enabled ACP for 1M+ merchants on launch day
- AI agents will browse, compare, and transact autonomously
- Businesses without machine-readable catalogs will be excluded from this channel

### Deliverables

1. **Commerce Schema Suite**
   - Product schema with pricing, availability, condition
   - Service schema with pricing and service area
   - Offer schema with price, valid dates, eligibility
   - OpeningHoursSpecification
   - AreaServed definitions
   - AggregateRating and individual Review schema

2. **Google Merchant Center Optimization**
   - Product feed audit and optimization
   - Structured data alignment with feed
   - Performance Max campaign readiness

3. **ACP/UCP Readiness Assessment**
   - API endpoint audit (can an AI agent query your catalog?)
   - Authentication and transaction flow review
   - Compatibility gap analysis

4. **AI Shopping Optimization**
   - ChatGPT Shopping visibility audit
   - Perplexity Shopping visibility audit
   - Product description optimization for AI consumption

### Positioning

"We're building your AI sales channel." This is not optimization -- it is channel development. Frame as comparable to building an e-commerce website in 2005 or launching on Amazon in 2010.

## Introductory Pricing (First 3 Clients)

First clients should receive reduced pricing to build case studies and testimonials:

- **Sprints**: $1,500-2,500 (not $3-5K)
- **Retainers**: Reduced rate for first 3 months
- **Audits**: Free or heavily discounted

The goal is proof, not revenue. First 3 clients provide:
- Before/after data for case studies
- Testimonials and quotes
- Process refinement (identify bottlenecks)
- Realistic time estimates for pricing

After 3 clients with documented results, raise to full pricing.

## Revenue Model

### Year 1 Target

| Source | Assumption | Monthly Revenue |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| Sprints | 2 sprints/month at $4K avg | $8,000 |
| Retainers | 5 clients at $1,500/mo avg | $7,500 |
| Audits | 4 audits/month at $750 avg | $3,000 |
| **Total** | | **$18,500/mo** |

### Unit Economics

- Sprint cost to deliver: ~5-8 hours (with automation) at $0 marginal labor cost (solo operator)
- API/tool costs per sprint: ~$20-50
- Retainer cost to serve: ~3-4 hours/month per client
- API/tool costs per retainer/month: ~$10-20
- Audit cost: ~$2-5 in API costs, 30 min manual review

Margins improve with every client as vertical intelligence compounds and automation workflows mature.
